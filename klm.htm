Gaming logos by Canva

As more and more people get into online gaming and esports, it’s time to upgrade your own game. Be part of the big league with the perfect logo for your brand or team. Whether you’re hosting a streaming channel or playing in an esports arena, you can stand out from your competition with cool gaming logo templates.

You may be a pro in gaming but a newbie in design. No worries—Canva is here to get you started with gaming logo ideas. Scroll through our templates for an arsenal of logos and choose one that best represents your brand.

You can customize the free gaming logo design template for your team in the dashboard. Are you looking for a wily wizard or a roaring tiger? You’ll find even more standout icons and graphics from our extensive media library, and you’ll be able to edit the colors to suit your theme. Better yet, why not upload the personalized icon your teammate designed during your earlier days? Match the perfect background for your logo. Choose gradients for the 3D effect, or settle with a flat backdrop for a modern and clean look.

Check out our font library for more bold and eye-catching styles. You’ll be sure to attract attention whether you’re using your logo template as your gaming channel’s thumbnail or as a large banner. Add a text element to sneak in your battle cry or catchphrase for that extra punch.

You can save your design as a high-resolution JPEG or remove the background and download a transparent PNG file. If you’ve added some of our animation effects, you can export it as a GIF. Upload your logo to your streaming account in the blink of an eye and watch your fans multiply. Soon, you’ll be able to create your own merchandise. Use your custom logo to design shirts and mugs in our editor and use Canva Print. Grow your army of supporters and watch your competition get owned even before you hit “play.”
